A single landing page usually takes 1–2 weeks from final Figma file to launch. A full multi-page site with CMS runs 3–5 weeks, depending on how much content review is needed on your side.

Both. Most clients come to me for the full Figma-to-Webflow process, but if you already have an approved design, I can build from that directly — it usually shortens the timeline by a week or more.
